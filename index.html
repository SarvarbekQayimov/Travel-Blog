About Japan

Japan, an island nation in East Asia, is known for its unique blend of ancient traditions and cutting-edge modernity. Explore breathtaking temples, experience vibrant cities, and indulge in world-renowned cuisine.

Capital

Tokyo

Language

Japanese

Currency

Yen (¥)

Top Attractions

Mount Fuji

Japan's iconic mountain and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Kyoto Temples

Explore centuries-old temples and serene gardens.

Tokyo

A vibrant metropolis offering a mix of tradition and innovation.

Travel Tips

  • Learn basic Japanese phrases for a better experience.
  • Carry cash as many small shops don't accept credit cards.
  • Use the Japan Rail Pass for convenient travel.
